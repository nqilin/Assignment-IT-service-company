    PRIME is an ERP software provider based in Cambodia, specializing in adaptable solutions suitable for any industry. With extensive experience, PRIME offers a comprehensive suite of ERP tools that enhance visibility across all business operations.

    We empower SMEs in Cambodia with a complete ERP ecosystem — from Accounting and Inventory to CRM and Manufacturing.
